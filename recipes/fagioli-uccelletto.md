  title: Fagioli all’uccelletto – Beans in Tomato Sauce

ingredients:
  - 1 pound cooked white beans, with a pint or so of their liquid
  - 1 large can of tomatoes
  - A few fresh sage leaves (dry would also work)
  - Garlic, as much as you like, chopped
  - Olive oil
---

Preheat the oven to 325. Add oil, garlic and sage to a pot or baking dish big enough to hold all the beans, and heat it for a minute. Add the tomatoes (and mash them with a potato masher if they're whole). Add the beans with their liquid and bring everything to a boil, then transfer it to the oven and cook uncovered until the sauce has reduced. After about an hour, give it a stir so more of the sauce gets direct heat.

Serve with toasted bread, or over pasta or polenta or on its own. Top with parmesan or mix in ricotta.
